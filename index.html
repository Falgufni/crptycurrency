Peer-to-Peer Transactions

Cryptocurrencies enable direct transactions between users (peer-to-peer) without the need for intermediaries like banks

Blockchain Technology

The blockchain is a decentralized and immutable ledger that records all transactions across a network.

Digital Assets

Cryptocurrencies exist purely in digital form and are typically represented by entries in a blockchain ledger. Each cryptocurrency has its own unit of value (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum) and can be stored in digital wallets.

Limited Supply

Many cryptocurrencies have a predetermined maximum supply, which can create scarcity and potentially influence their value. For example, Bitcoin has a maximum supply of 21 million coins.
